_This score grades the "Storefront" health of your channel page. Fix these once, and reap the benefits forever._

_**1\. Description SEO (20 Points)**_

*   _**How we check it:**_ _We ensure your Channel "About" section is robust (over 300 characters) and clearly explains your niche._
    
*   _**Why it matters:**_ _Your channel description gets indexed by Google. A keyword-rich description helps your actual channel page rank when people search for topics in your niche._
    

_**2\. Homepage Layout & Shelves (20 Points)**_

*   _**How we check it:**_ _We verify that you have active featured sections (Playlists, Popular Videos, etc.) turned on for your channel homepage._
    
*   _**Why it matters:**_ _A raw feed of your latest uploads is visually overwhelming. Curated playlists ("Start Here," "Best Of") turn casual visitors into binge-watchers._
    

_**3\. Trailer / Featured Video (20 Points)**_

*   _**How we check it:**_ _We check if you have set a Channel Trailer for unsubscribed visitors or a Featured Video for returning subscribers._
    
*   _**Why it matters:**_ _Auto-playing a highly engaging video when someone visits your page is the single highest-converting tool YouTube provides to turn a profile visitor into a subscriber._
    

_**4\. Channel Handle (10 Points)**_

*   _**How we check it:**_ _We check if you have claimed a custom @handle._
    
*   _**Why it matters:**_ _Handles are YouTube's modern URL structure. They are absolutely essential for viewers to tag you, for Shorts discovery, and for protecting your brand identity._
    

_**5\. Video Watermark (10 Points)**_

*   _**How we check it:**_ _We check your branding settings to ensure you have uploaded a video watermark._
    
*   _**Why it matters:**_ _This places a tiny "Subscribe" button in the bottom right corner of every single video you ever upload. It is a massive driver of passive subscriptions._
    

_**6\. Visual Branding (10 Points)**_

*   _**How we check it:**_ _We verify that your channel has a custom uploaded Banner image and a Custom Avatar._
    
*   _**Why it matters:**_ _Your banner is your billboard. A missing or default banner silently communicates to viewers and sponsors that the channel might be abandoned or amateur._
    

_**7\. Business & Social Links (10 Points)**_

*   _**How we check it:**_ _We scan your channel profile for linked social media accounts, websites, or a public business inquiry email._
    
*   _**Why it matters:**_ _Missing contact info means missing out on brand deals. Missing social links means trapping your audience on YouTube instead of building a resilient, multi-platform business._
    

#### _**🚨 Channel Setup Penalties**_

*   _**The Ghost Town Penalty (-15 Points):**_ _You haven't published a new public video in the last 90 days. Why: If the algorithm thinks you quit, your channel's organic reach gets severely throttled._
    
*   _**Dead Link Penalty (-10 Points):**_ _A social or website link on your channel profile returns a 404 error. Why: This looks incredibly unprofessional to brands looking to sponsor you._
    
*   _**Default Avatar Penalty (-10 Points):**_ _You are still using the default Google-generated initial letter as a profile picture. Why: This instantly kills credibility and makes your channel look like a bot account._
    
*   _**Missing Contact Email (-5 Points):**_ _You don't have a public business inquiry email listed. Why: You are literally leaving money on the table from sponsors who can't figure out how to contact you._
